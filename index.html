The story of TADC 🃏!

The amazing digital circus is a dark and mysterious animated story about a group of humans trapped inside a strange virtual world that looks like a colorful circus. The main character, Pomni, suddenly finds herself in this digital place after putting on a headset,
and she loses her real identity. Inside the circus, she meets other trapped people who have been there for a long time, all controlled by an unpredictable
artificial intelligence named Caine. Although the world seems fun and playful,
it hides deep fear and sadness, as none of them can escape. Throughout the story, the characters go on strange adventures while
trying to understand their situation and find a way out, making the series a mix of comedy, horror, and psychological drama
